# Brand Identity Quick Reference

## Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Clear** | Say what we mean, no jargon |
| **Calm** | Confident without being loud |
| **Helpful** | Lead with usefulness |
| **Human** | Sound like people, not a corporation |
| **Honest** | No overpromising, no vague language |

---

## Core Values (One-Liners)

1. **Clarity Over Complexity** — Make technology feel simple
2. **Fit Over Features** — Solve problems, don't sell tools
3. **Partnership Over Projects** — Build relationships, not just deliverables
4. **Craftsmanship Over Speed** — Build things right
5. **Honesty Over Sales** — Trust over transactions

---

---

## Writing Principles

- **Lead with outcomes**, not features
- **Be specific**, not generic
- **Respect their intelligence** — they're smart, just busy
- **Keep it scannable** — short paragraphs, clear headings, bullet points

---

---

## Visual Design Principles

**Spacing:**
- Whitespace is a feature, not wasted space
- Section padding: 80–120px vertical
- Content max-width: 1200px

**Imagery:**
- Authentic over stock
- Documentary-style photos
- Simple, geometric icons

**UI:**
- Subtle shadow OR border on cards—not both
- Generous padding (24–32px)
- Clear hover/focus states

## Target Audience Snapshot

**Who:** SMBs with high volumes of repetitive work

**Industries:** Trades, service businesses, property management, professional services, local retail

**The person:** A business owner who is good at what they do but stretched thin, wants a partner not a vendor

**What they feel:** Overwhelmed, frustrated by software that almost works, skeptical but open
